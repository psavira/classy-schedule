How do I make my schedule?

First, make sure you are logged in. Once you have logged into Classy-Schedule, navigate to the top navigation bar and click on "Class Entry". This will navigate you to a new page where you will enter your class details. Make sure to click submit. Follow this same process but click on "Classroom Entry". Finally, Click on "Schedule" and choose which classes you want in each time slot. Click on save progress and navigate to a new room using the drop-down button at the bottom of the page. Follow the same process till you have finished and then click view schedule!
